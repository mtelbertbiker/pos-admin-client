Fee Machine - Time based Fee Calculation Tools for Billiards, Bars, Breweries, and Restaurants

Fee Machine calculates minute by minute fees for any item by time of day, day of week, and number of players.

Designed for Billiard Operators, Breweries, Bars, and Restaurants looking to grow their gaming revenues. Provides consistent fee calculation and tracking.

With Fee Machine, you can define custom rate structures for each type of item you rent and how you rent them, such as Daily, League, and Party rates for Pool, Daily for Shuffleboard, Daily and League for Darts.

You can define custom rates for number of players, such as separate rates for 1 to 2 players, 3 to 4 players, 4 to 6 players, etc.

Fee Machine runs on Android tablets. It uses simple barcode integration with leading Restaurant POS systems such as Aloha, which means it’s easy to setup and use!

Wireless Light Control available - turn lights on/off with each rental!
