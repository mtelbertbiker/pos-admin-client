Fee Machine - Product Features

This tells how the product works

Wireless Billiard Light Control is a optional feature of Fee Machine. When enabled, Fee Machine will turn on a light above your Billiard Table at the start of each rental, and turn it off at the end, providing an easy way to monitor which items are being actively rented.
